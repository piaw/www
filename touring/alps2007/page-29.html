Appendix E: About this Report

This trip report was written on the flights from Zurich to San Francisco on a Lenova Thinkpad X60. The Lenova has such a good battery life running Emacs that I had an hour of battery life left when I finished the initial draft of this report. The resulting web pages were produced by a custom python script. Pictures were processed by Picasa, and uploaded to PicasaWebfor hosting.
