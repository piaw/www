This is the tour report for my 2007 Tour of the Swiss, Italian, and Austrian Alps, which I did with Mike Samuel, Roberto Peon, and my brother, Sy Na. We registered 1501km of cycling and 33047m of climbing, in 16 riding days. We had 4 flat tires (1 self-inflicted), and one shredded tire, though the airline did bend Mike's rear deraileur hanger.

The original proposal (sent to an internal mailing list) can be viewed here. Those who expressed interest received an information packet a little bit later. A few cycling trips ensued before the trip departure that can be perused on my blog.
