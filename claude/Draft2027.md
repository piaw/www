# Alps 2027 — Draft Tour Plan

**Munich → Dolomites → Stelvio → Livigno → Bernina → Splügen → [FORK] → Munich**

Status: draft. Tandem with Boen (12) as stoker. **No support vehicle** — everything carried on the bikes, all transfers by train. Bowen likely not on this trip (high school).

**30 days either way.** The route forks at Splügen (Day 20/21). Both branches live.

---

## Design principles (carried from 2026)

1. **Fixed bases, optional rides.** The Hospental model: lock lodging for multiple nights, let weather decide which rides happen. No floating "weather spare days" — resilience lives in the structure.
2. **Train the segments with no cycling value.** Ride what's worth riding.
3. **Flex booking.** Phone from the road mid-afternoon, 1–2 nights ahead. **Pre-booking this far out is bad policy.** Two exceptions only, both on the South branch (see below).
4. **Paved corridors for the loaded tandem.** Cross-check Google Maps paved routing against RWGPS/Komoot, which snap to Veloland/unpaved connectors (2026 Col des Mosses).
5. **No support vehicle.** Multi-night bases mean luggage sits still on rest days and bonus rides.
6. **Captain power 100–125W NP on big cols.** Proven range across the entire 2026 tour.

### ⚠ What South actually costs

**Two hard bookings, made ~11 months out** — against a standing preference not to pre-book:

1. **Zurich → Munich EC.** Bike reservation, limited spaces, cannot be got late.
2. **Rosenlaui ×3.** No substitutes in the valley. **This is the booking that forecloses North** — the Zurich ticket alone still leaves a Chur → Zurich fallback, but Rosenlaui is three nights in the Haslital that North never reaches. If there's real doubt, book Zurich first and Rosenlaui later.

**The unslackable window: Days 21–23.** Splügen → Bellinzona → Nufenen (2,478m) → Grimsel (2,164m) → Rosenlaui. Two high passes, three days, an unmovable paid booking at the end — and all the slack (Gardena, Bormio) sits *upstream* of Splügen, so it can't help.

**Bailout if a day is lost in the window:** Grimsel PostBus, with the tandem taken apart. Not enjoyable, but possible. **Not a cliff.**

**Buffer:** schedule to reach **Innertkirchen or Meiringen (~625m)** the night before the Rosenlaui booking starts. Real lodging, an hour below Rosenlaui, reachable from Grimsel. Clean window → a pleasant Haslital evening. Lost day → already absorbed. This is insurance; the PostBus stays theoretical.

### Train segments — the pattern
Trains are used for exactly the stretches with no cycling value:
- **S-Bahn → Holzkirchen** (flat-inducing Isar path)
- **Innsbruck → Brenner summit** (motorway corridor)
- **Bruneck → Toblach** (dull Pustertal drag)
- **Merano → Spondinig/Prad** (boring Vinschgau false-flat)
- *North:* Thusis → Tiefencastel (busy Schinstrasse) · Innsbruck → Scharnitz (Zirler Berg, finish C)
